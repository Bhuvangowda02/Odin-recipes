Patty Burger

Description

This classic patty burger is juicy and flavorful, made with a perfectly seasoned beef patty, fresh toppings, and a soft burger bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix gently to combine.
  3. Form the mixture into four equal patties, making a slight indentation in the center of each patty with your thumb to prevent puffing up during cooking.
  4. Grill or cook the patties in the skillet for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in the skillet until lightly browned.
  7. Assemble the burgers: Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by the patty with melted cheese, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles.
  8. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top bun and place it on top of the burger.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious patty burger!
